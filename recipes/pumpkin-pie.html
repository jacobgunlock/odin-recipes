Pumpkin Pie

Description

You tryna make some pumpkin pie!? I got you just read on below.

Ingredients

Pumpkin Pie
  1. 1 (9 Inch) unbaked pie crust
  2. 1 (15 Oz) can pumpkin puree
  3. 1 (14 Oz) can sweetened condensed milk
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 1 tspn pumpkin pie spice
Vanilla Meringue
  1. 1 cup granulated sugar
  2. 5 large egg whites
  3. 1/4 tspn cream of tartar
  4. 1 pinch kosher salt
  5. 2 tspns pure vanilla extract
